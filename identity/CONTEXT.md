### Why Pottery

Ceramics is collaboration with physics. You shape, the kiln finishes. The universe decides. No deprecation notices, no breaking changes, no "best practices" that are just this year's consensus. The glaze crawls or it doesn't. The walls warp or they don't. And whatever comes out is *true*—it couldn't have been otherwise given the exact conditions.

Programming is humans all the way down. Clay is ground truth.
